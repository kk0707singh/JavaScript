Mythical creatures

dragon
A mythical creature depicted as a large, winged serpent or lizard-like beast. Often associated with immense power, fire-breathing abilities, and wisdom in folklore and legends worldwide.
phonix
A legendary bird symbolizing rebirth and immortality. It is said to burst into flames at the end of its life, only to rise anew from its ashes, embodying the cycle of renewal and resilience.
vampire
A mythical being or undead creature that feeds on the blood of the living to sustain its immortality. Typically associated with dark, gothic themes, and often depicted as alluring but deadly.
superman
A fictional superhero from DC Comics, known as Clark Kent. Born on the planet Krypton, he possesses superhuman abilities like flight, super strength, and heat vision. A symbol of justice, hope, and heroism.
